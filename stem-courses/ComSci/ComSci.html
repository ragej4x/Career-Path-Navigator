About the Course

BS Computer Science is a rigorous program focused on the theoretical foundations of computing, algorithms, software development, and advanced computational problem-solving. Students study programming languages, data structures, algorithms, artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer architecture, operating systems, and software engineering principles. The program emphasizes mathematical reasoning, analytical thinking, and innovation in developing cutting-edge software solutions, preparing graduates for careers in software development, research, and technological innovation.
