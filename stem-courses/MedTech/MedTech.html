About the Course

A Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology (BSMT) is a four-year undergraduate program focused on laboratory diagnostics, training students in clinical chemistry, hematology, microbiology, blood banking, and more, culminating in a year-long internship to prepare them as licensed Medical Technologists (MTs) for roles in disease detection, prevention, and treatment in various healthcare settings
