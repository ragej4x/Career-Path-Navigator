About the Course

A Bachelor of Science (BS) in Chemistry is a four-year undergraduate degree providing a strong foundation in core chemical principles (organic, inorganic, physical, analytical, biochemistry) and lab skills, preparing graduates for roles in industry (R&D, quality control, manufacturing), government, academia, or further study in medicine or law, with many programs leading to eligibility for the Professional Chemist Licensure Examination. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on research, modern instrumentation, and problem-solving, equipping students for diverse careers or graduate programs
