About the Course

A Bachelor of Science (BS) in Applied Mathematics is a versatile degree focused on using mathematical principles and models to solve real-world problems in fields like tech, finance, engineering, and data science, bridging theory with practical application through subjects like modeling, statistics, programming, and optimization. Graduates develop strong analytical, problem-solving, and computational skills, preparing them for careers in data analytics, operations research, actuarial science, and further academic pursuits.
