About the Course

A Bachelor of Science (BS) in Physics is a 4-year (or sometimes 5-year) undergraduate degree providing deep understanding of matter, energy, space, and time, covering mechanics, electromagnetism, quantum mechanics, and experimental skills, preparing graduates for careers in research, tech, healthcare, finance, education, or further graduate studies through rigorous math, analysis, and thesis work. Programs often offer specializations like materials science, medical physics, or engineering physics, focusing on practical applications.
