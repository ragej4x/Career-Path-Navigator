About the Course

BS Information Systems bridges technology and business, focusing on designing, implementing, and managing information systems that support organizational goals. Students learn database management, systems analysis and design, enterprise resource planning, business process management, IT project management, and business intelligence. The program emphasizes understanding business requirements and translating them into effective technological solutions, preparing graduates to serve as the critical link between IT departments and business units.
