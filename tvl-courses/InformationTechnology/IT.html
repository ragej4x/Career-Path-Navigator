About the Course

An Information Technology (IT) college course, like a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), teaches students how to use computer software and hardware to create, manage, and maintain IT systems for businesses and organizations. The curriculum includes areas like programming, web development, database management, network administration, and cybersecurity, with some programs offering specializations in areas like mobile and web applications. The goal is to train professionals who can solve problems and meet organizational needs using IT.
