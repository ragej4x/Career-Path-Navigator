About the Course

BS Culinary Management combines culinary arts expertise with business management skills. Students master advanced cooking techniques, international cuisines, pastry and baking, food presentation, while also learning restaurant operations, menu engineering, cost control, inventory management, kitchen leadership, and entrepreneurship. The program includes extensive hands-on training in commercial kitchens, food service operations, and culinary innovation, preparing graduates to become executive chefs, restaurant owners, culinary consultants, or food business entrepreneurs.
