About the Course

BS Cosmetic Science is a specialized program that combines chemistry, biology, and technology to develop, formulate, and test cosmetic and personal care products. Students study organic chemistry, biochemistry, dermatology, formulation science, quality control, regulatory affairs, and product development. The program emphasizes hands-on laboratory work in creating skincare, haircare, makeup, and fragrance products, while ensuring safety, efficacy, and compliance with industry regulations. Graduates are prepared for careers in beauty and personal care industries.
