Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW)

Explore the world of metal fabrication, welding techniques, and industrial craftsmanship.

Track Overview

Shielded Metal Arc Welding focuses on welding processes used in construction, manufacturing, metal repair, and fabrication. Students gain hands-on training using welding machines, learning proper handling, metal preparation, safety procedures, and welding techniques for various types of joints and positions.
