Computer System Servicing

Explore the world of computer repair, maintenance, and technical support.

Track Overview

Computer System Servicing focuses on diagnosing, assembling, and maintaining computer systems and networks. Students gain hands-on experience in troubleshooting hardware and software issues, configuring networks, and ensuring systems run efficiently.
