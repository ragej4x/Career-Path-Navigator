GRADE 11 ABM SUBJECTS

General Mathematics

General Mathematics is a core subject in the Senior High School curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd). It is designed to strengthen students’ understanding of essential mathematical concepts and enhance their analytical and problem-solving skills through real-life applications. The subject focuses on topics such as functions and their representations, rational, exponential, and logarithmic expressions, and basic business mathematics including interest, loans, and annuities. These lessons help students apply mathematical reasoning in practical situations such as financial decision-making and everyday problem solving.

Organization Management

Organization and Management is a specialized subject under the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand of the Senior High School curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd). The subject introduces students to the fundamental principles, functions, and processes involved in managing organizations effectively. It covers key topics such as planning, organizing, leading, and controlling, as well as organizational structures, management theories, decision-making, and human resource management. Students learn how managers coordinate people and resources to achieve organizational goals in business and non-business settings. Through real-world examples and practical applications, the subject helps learners develop leadership skills, critical thinking, and an understanding of how organizations operate. This course prepares ABM students for higher education and future careers in business, entrepreneurship, and management.

Earth and Life Science

Earth and Life Science is a core subject in the Senior High School curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd). The subject introduces students to basic concepts related to the Earth’s systems and living organisms, emphasizing how natural processes affect life on Earth. It covers topics such as the origin and structure of the Earth, minerals and rocks, weather and climate, ecosystems, biodiversity, and biological evolution. Students learn how the Earth’s physical processes interact with living things and how human activities impact the environment. Through scientific inquiry, observation, and real-world applications, the subject develops students’ critical thinking and environmental awareness. Earth and Life Science prepares learners for advanced science subjects and promotes responsible stewardship of the environment.

Business Mathematics

Business Mathematics is a specialized subject under the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand in the Senior High School curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd). The subject focuses on the application of mathematical concepts in business and financial decision-making. It covers topics such as markup and markdown, simple and compound interest, discounts, profit and loss, taxation, loans, and annuities. Through real-world business scenarios, students learn how to compute costs, set prices, analyze profits, and manage financial transactions. This subject develops students’ numerical skills, analytical thinking, and practical understanding of business operations. Business Mathematics prepares ABM learners for higher education, entrepreneurship, and careers in accounting, finance, and management.

Media and information Literacy

Media and Information Literacy is a core subject in the Senior High School curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd). The subject equips students with the skills needed to access, analyze, evaluate, and create information across different media platforms. It covers topics such as types of media, information sources, media messages, ethical and responsible use of information, and digital citizenship. Students learn how media influences society and how to identify credible information, misinformation, and bias. Through critical thinking and practical activities, the subject helps learners become responsible consumers and producers of information. Media and Information Literacy prepares students to participate effectively in the digital world and make informed decisions in their academic and everyday lives.

Oral Communication

Oral Communication is a core subject in the Senior High School curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd). The subject focuses on developing students’ speaking and listening skills for effective communication in different contexts. It covers topics such as communication models, verbal and nonverbal communication, speech styles, speech delivery, and listening strategies. Students learn how to express ideas clearly, participate in discussions, and deliver speeches with confidence and purpose. Through practical activities and real-life situations, the subject enhances students’ confidence, critical thinking, and interpersonal skills. Oral Communication prepares learners for academic presentations, workplace communication, and everyday interactions.

Komunikasyon at Pananaliksik sa Wika at Kulturang Pilipino

Komunikasyon at Pananaliksik sa Wika at Kulturang Pilipino ay isang core subject sa Senior High School na itinakda ng Department of Education (DepEd). Layunin ng asignaturang ito na malinang ang kakayahan ng mga mag-aaral sa epektibong paggamit ng wikang Filipino sa iba’t ibang sitwasyong pangkomunikasyon at akademiko. Tinalakay sa asignaturang ito ang kalikasan at kasaysayan ng wika, mga konsepto ng komunikasyon, wika at kultura, at mga batayang kasanayan sa pananaliksik. Natututuhan ng mga mag-aaral ang wastong pangangalap ng impormasyon, pagsulat ng akademikong papel, at pagsusuri ng mga isyung may kaugnayan sa lipunang Pilipino. Sa pamamagitan ng kritikal na pag-iisip at makabuluhang gawain, nahahasa ang kakayahan ng mga mag-aaral sa pananaliksik at komunikasyon. Inihahanda ng asignaturang ito ang mga mag-aaral sa mas mataas na antas ng pag-aaral at sa aktibong pakikilahok sa lipunan bilang responsableng mamamayan.

understanding Society Culture and Politics

Understanding Culture, Society, and Politics is a core subject in the Senior High School curriculum prescribed by the Department of Education (DepEd). The subject introduces students to the basic concepts of culture, society, and political structures, and how these shape human behavior and social relationships. It covers topics such as cultural diversity, social institutions, socialization, power and authority, governance, and citizenship. Students learn how societies are organized, how cultures influence identity, and how political systems affect individuals and communities. Through analysis of real-world issues and social situations, the subject develops students’ critical thinking, social awareness, and sense of responsibility. Understanding Culture, Society, and Politics prepares learners to become informed, active, and responsible members of society.
