About the Course

A BS in Management Engineering (ME) is an interdisciplinary degree blending engineering principles, systems thinking, and business management to create leaders who solve complex organizational problems using data and technology, focusing on process optimization, analytics, and strategic decision-making for roles in tech, consulting, finance, and operations. It's distinct from general management by emphasizing a strong technical/analytical foundation, preparing graduates for roles bridging engineering and business strategy, like supply chain, project management, and product management.
