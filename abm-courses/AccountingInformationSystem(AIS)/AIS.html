About the Course

Bachelor of Science in Accounting Information System (BSAIS) integrates accounting, business, and information technology. The program focuses on the design, implementation, and management of computerized accounting and financial systems. Students develop skills in financial accounting, auditing, taxation, systems analysis, database management, and enterprise systems to support data-driven business decisions.
