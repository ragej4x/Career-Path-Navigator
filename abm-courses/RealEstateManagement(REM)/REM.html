About the Course

A Bachelor of Science in Real Estate Management (BSREM) is a 4-year program that prepares graduates for careers in property brokerage, appraisal, development, and consultancy, blending business principles with real estate laws and practices, leading to licensure exams for brokers, appraisers, and consultants. Graduates can work in various roles like property manager, sales agent, investment analyst, or government assessor, focusing on property valuation, investment, marketing, and legal aspects.
