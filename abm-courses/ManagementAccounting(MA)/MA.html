About the Course

Bachelor of Science in Management Accounting is a business-oriented accounting program that focuses on financial planning, cost management, performance evaluation, and strategic decision-making. The course equips students with skills in budgeting, financial analysis, cost accounting, internal control, and business strategy to support management in achieving organizational goals.
