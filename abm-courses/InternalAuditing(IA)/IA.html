About the Course

A Bachelor of Science in Internal Auditing (BSIA) is a specialized 4-year degree program, common in the Philippines, that trains professionals to provide objective assurance and consulting to improve an organization's risk management, control, and governance processes, covering ethics, financial analysis, compliance, and IT auditing to prepare graduates for roles like. Graduates develop skills in risk assessment, fraud detection (Forensic/Fraud Examiner), internal controls, and strategic analysis, often leading to certifications like the CIA and careers in corporate governance, risk management, or operations.
